About

Welcome to the Gardening from then Heart Club! Together, we can spread more joy (and pollen) through gardening. I began gardening in 2020 and it was the best decision that I could have made for my mental health. It allowed my mind to slow down and enjoy God's wonderful creation. Gardening brings a certain peace over my mind, body, and spirit and has become something I look forward to each Spring and Summer. I'm excited for you to start your gardening journey!
