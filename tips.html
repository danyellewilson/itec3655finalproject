Gardening from the Heart

1. Roses thrive and flourish when given potassium. I will save the peels from 4-5 bananas in the fridge, then blend them all with a bit of water, then pour directly on the base of each of my rose bushes. It's a cheap yet effective approach to a healthy rose bush.
