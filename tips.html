Gardening from the Heart

To beginners, I recommend browsing the clearance section at your local greenhouse or Lowe's. This approach allows you to test your green thumb with little investment. Beginning with a hydrangea from the clearance section, I learned how to properly take care of it. It's an excellent way to start your gardening journey!
